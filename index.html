Grace is a Miami native representing five generations of local legacy and a dedicated pre-dental student committed to expanding access to oral healthcare. As the founding president of Global Dental Brigades, she leads international service initiatives focused on sustainable dental care and community education in underserved areas.

On campus, Grace serves as a Peer-Led Team Learning workshop leader and a teaching assistant for First Year Directions, mentoring students through their academic and personal transitions. She is also active in Student Government and Greek life, recently completing her term as Vice President of Member Development for Alpha Delta Pi. Recognized with the R. Marilyn Jones Memorial Scholarship and as an Order of Omega tappee, Grace is driven by a passion for dentistry, leadership, and service both locally and globally.
